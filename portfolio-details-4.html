Pig Game

Pig is a dice game for two+ players. Each turn, roll the die and accumulate points. Roll a one, lose points and pass your turn. Risk or play it safe to reach 100 points first, and win the game.
Making of Game: Being a game rendered by the web browser, it is built by the help of HTML, CSS (for the Front-end) and JavaScript (for the Back-end).
